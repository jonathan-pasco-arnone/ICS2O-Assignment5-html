Identify the educational institution

I would like to attend Carleton University to earn a degree in Computer Science to become a programmer.


Entrance requirements

Entrance Requirement for Computer Science at Carleton University:

  • Six grade 12 courses in university preparation or mixed level
  • Advanced Functions (MHF4U) or Calculus and Vector (MCV4U)
  • An average of 80%-82% in top grade 12 U/M courses (must include either MHF4U or MCV4U)


Description of first year program (courses) that you would take

Computer Science Streams and First Year Courses:
If I take computer science at carleton university, I can choose from 7 streams: Algorithms, Computer and Internet security,
Computer Game Development, Management and Business Systems, Mobile Computing, Network Computing, and Software
Engineering (Computer Science).
If I choose to do the mobile computing stream, my first year courses will include the following classes:

  • Introduction to Computer Science I
  • Introduction to Computer Science II
  • Discrete Structures I

  • Elementary Calculus I
